My Priorities

Data Analysis & Visualization

Although I specialize in program analytics and project controls, I enjoy analyzing data of all types. I'm particularly interested in identifying and tracking business KPIs, as well as finding interesting implications of macroeconomic and socioeconomic data.

Business Process Improvement

I find both 'hard' work and 'smart' work gratifying. I have learned the best way to improve on a process is to master the basics before making improvements or incorporating technology, so that hard work gets smarter faster.

Project Management

I still have a lot to learn about project management, but the challenges I've faced so far have been a good opportunity for personal and professional growth. When a team is brought together to achieve a common goal, the results are satisfying.

Administrative Support

I'm comfortable supporting accounting, project management, finance, business development, proposal support, data entry, and more. Sometimes utilizing human capital is the best way forward, and I appreciate the nostalgia of revisiting where many of us got our start.
